Fresas Con Crema

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups of Sour Cream
  • 1/2 Cup of Evaporated Milk
  • 3/4 Cup of Sweetened Condensed Milk
  • 1 tsp. of Pure Vanilla Extract
  • 1 lb of fresh strawberries

Directions

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together sour cream, evapoated milk, sweetened condensed
    milk and vanilla until well blended and no lumps remain.
    Place in refrigerator until ready to use.
  2. Remove tops from strawberries and cut strawberries into bite sized pieces.
  3. Spoon strawberries into 5 cups or bowls. Pour sweet cream over berries and serve with a spoon.

Leftover cream base can be stored in the refrigerator while tightly covered for a few days.

Chicken Fettuccine Alfredo

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs Chicken Breast
  • 3/4 lbs Fettuccine Pasta
  • 1 lb of Small Onion, Finely Chopped
  • 3 Cloves of Garlic, Minced
  • 3 1/2 cups of Half and Half
  • 1/4 Cups of Parsley, Finely Chopped
  • Salt and Pepper to Taste
  • 3 Tbsp of Olive Oil divided
  • 1 Tbsp of Butter

Directions

  1. Cook fettuccini in a pot of salted water according to package instructions.
    Drain and set Aside.
  2. Slice chicken into strips and season with salt and pepper.
    In a large skillet, heat 2 Tbsp of olive oil over medium to high heat and saute chicken until lightly golden and cooked through.
    Remove chicken from the pan and cover to keep warm.
  3. In the same pan over medium to high heat, add 1 Tbsp of olive oil and 1 Tbsp of butter.
    Add onion and saute until soft. Add garlic and saute for 30 seconds, stirring constantly.
  4. Add half and half and simmer over medium to high heat for 8 to 10 minutes or until sauce thickens.
    Add chicken into the pan and add parsley and season to taste.
  5. Add cooked pasta and stir to combine. Heat for another minute until warmed then turn off the heat.
    Cover and let rest for about 10 minutes then stir and serve.

Snickerdoodles

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Cup of Softened Butter
  • 1/2 Cup of Shortening
  • 1 1/2 cups of Granulated Sugar
  • 2 Eggs
  • 2 tsp. of Vanilla Extract
  • 2 3/4 Cups of All-Purpose Flour
  • 2 tsp. of Cream of Tartar
  • 1 tsp. of Baking Soda
  • 1/4 tsp. of salt
  • 2 Tbsp. of Granulated Sugar
  • 2 tsp. of Ground Cinnamon

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Cream together butter, shortening, 1 1/2 cups of granulated sugar, eggs, and vanilla.
    Blend in flour, cream of tartar, soda, and salt. Shape dough by rounded spoonfuls into balls.
  3. Mix 2 tablespoons of sugar and cinnamon together.Roll balls of dough in mixture.
    Place 2 inches apart on ungreased baking sheets.
  4. Bake 8 - 10 minutes or until set but not too hard. Remove immediately from baking sheets.
